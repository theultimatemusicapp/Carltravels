Paris

History

Paris, often dubbed "The City of Light," has a history that dates back over 2,000 years. It has been a center of art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Significant events, such as the French Revolution and the construction of iconic landmarks, have shaped its rich heritage.

Population

As of 2023, Paris has a population of approximately 2.1 million people within the city limits, with a metropolitan area housing over 12 million residents.

Notable Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Champs-Élysées
  • Sacre-Coeur Basilica

Best Time to Visit

The best times to visit Paris are in the spring (April to June) and fall (September to November) when the weather is mild and the city is less crowded.

Prices Overview

Paris can be relatively expensive. Accommodation ranges from budget hotels at €70 per night to luxury hotels exceeding €300 per night. Dining out can vary from €15 for a casual meal to €50+ at high-end restaurants.

Fun Facts

  • Paris has more museums than any other city in the world.
  • The Louvre is the most visited museum globally, with over 10 million visitors annually.
  • There are approximately 29,000 buildings in Paris.
