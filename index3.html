Essential Travel Tips

Practical advice from years of globetrotting to help you travel smarter.

Document Safety

Always keep digital copies of important documents and share your itinerary with someone back home.

Money Matters

Carry multiple payment options and always have some local currency for emergencies.

Local Phrases

Learn basic greetings and phrases in the local language—it opens doors and hearts.
