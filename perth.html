Perth

History

Perth, the capital of Western Australia, was founded in 1829 as a free settlement. It grew rapidly during the gold rushes of the late 19th century and has since developed into a vibrant modern city known for its stunning beaches and outdoor lifestyle.

Population

As of 2023, Perth has a population of approximately 2.1 million people, making it the fourth most populous city in Australia.

Notable Attractions

  • Kings Park and Botanic Garden
  • Cottesloe Beach
  • Perth Zoo
  • The Perth Mint
  • Swan River

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Perth is during the spring (September to November) and autumn (March to May) when the weather is warm and pleasant.

Prices Overview

Fun Facts

  • Perth is the sunniest capital city in Australia, enjoying over 8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
  • The city is home to the largest inner-city park in the world, Kings Park.
  • Perth is known for its unique wildlife, including the quokka, often called the "world's happiest animal."
