Warsaw

Home Destinations

What to See

The Royal Castle, Old Town Market Square, Łazienki Park, and the Warsaw Uprising Museum are must-visit spots.

A Brief History of the City

Warsaw is a city with a tumultuous history, including being heavily impacted during World War II. It has since rebuilt itself into a thriving metropolis.

Top 10 Attractions

  • The Royal Castle
  • Łazienki Park
  • Warsaw Uprising Museum
  • Old Town Market Square
  • Palace of Culture and Science
  • Warsaw Zoo
  • POLIN Museum of the History of Polish Jews
  • Praga District
  • Wilanów Palace
  • Vistula River Beaches

What to Bring

Comfortable walking shoes, a camera, a city guidebook, and weather-appropriate clothing.

Currency

Polish Zloty (PLN)

Best Time to Visit

Late spring and early autumn for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

Fun Facts

Warsaw’s Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and was meticulously reconstructed after World War II.
