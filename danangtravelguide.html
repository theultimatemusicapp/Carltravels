3-Day Da Nang Itinerary

Day 1: Beaches & City Vibes

  • Morning: Relax at My Khe Beach; grab a coconut.
  • Afternoon: Explore Dragon Bridge and Han River.
  • Evening: Dine at local seafood stalls on An Thuong street.

Day 2: Marble Mountains & Hoi An

  • Morning: Hike Marble Mountains; visit caves and pagodas.
  • Afternoon: Day trip to Hoi An (30 min by taxi); explore ancient town.
  • Evening: Lantern-lit dinner in Hoi An; return to Da Nang.

Day 3: Ba Na Hills Adventure

  • Full Day: Visit Ba Na Hills; ride cable car, explore Golden Bridge.
  • Evening: Chill at a beachfront bar on My Khe.
